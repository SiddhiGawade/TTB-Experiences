# Verification Report: Krabi Travel Recommendation Guide

## Summary & Quality Score
Score: 95% - Pass with Minor Revision
The guide is excellent and well-researched, with a very high degree of factual accuracy regarding weather, food, and logistics. However, there is one minor outdated fact regarding a local attraction that needs to be updated.

## Verified Claims
- **Weather:** October climate data (highs 28-31°C, lows 24°C, monsoon tail end) is accurate.
- **Activities & Sightseeing:** Details for Railay Beach, 4-Island Tour, Emerald Pool (including arrival advice), and hidden gems (Tha Pom Khlong Song Nam, Ao Thalane, Khaothong Hill, Kuan Nom Saow Viewpoint) are all verified as accurate.
- **Shopping & Markets:** Krabi Town Walking Street (Soi Maharaj 8, Fri-Sun 5-10 PM) and Ao Nang Landmark Night Market locations and hours are accurate. Maharaj Market is indeed a morning market.
- **Dining:** The descriptions of Khao Yam, Massaman Curry, and Tom Kha are correct. All listed vegetarian (Govinda's, "Jay" stalls) and Indian restaurants (Bawarchi Delight, Bombay Palace) are accurately located in Ao Nang with correct specialties.
- **Logistics:** Information on SIM cards and local ride-hailing apps (Grab, Bolt, inDrive, 12GoAsia) is correct.
