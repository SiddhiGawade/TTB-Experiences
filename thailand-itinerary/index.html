Day 01 & 02

Day 01: Arrive at Chiang Mai & Drop to Hotel

Upon your arrival at Chiang Mai International Airport, our representative will warmly welcome you with a meet-and-greet service. You will then be transferred to your hotel for check-in and some time to relax. In the evening, enjoy a delightful welcome dinner by the riverside at The Gallery Restaurant, offering the perfect introduction to the charm of Chiang Mai.

Overnight stay in Chiang Mai.

Day 2: City Temple Tour of Chiang Mai

Start your day with a delicious buffet breakfast at the hotel before setting out on a guided city tour of Chiang Mai.

Drive through the city's vibrant markets, charming residential neighborhoods, and bustling main streets while discovering its rich cultural heritage.

Visit some of Chiang Mai's most iconic temples, including Wat Phra Singh, Wat Chedi Luang, Wat Chiang Man, and Wat Suan Dok. After the tour, return to your hotel and enjoy the rest of the evening at leisure.

Overnight stay in Chiang Mai.
